OUR APPROACH

Two different worlds,
handled precisely

Facilities management and physical security sit on genuinely different regulatory footing. We never blur the two.

Professional, Not Statutory
Facilities & Property Management
No single statutory body governs facilities management in South Africa. Our team pursues recognised professional designations — SAFMA, SAPOA, and IREM's Certified Property Manager — as a genuine competency standard.
Credibility through real professional development, never overstated as legal registration.
Statutorily Regulated
Physical Security Coordination
The Private Security Industry Regulation Act requires PSIRA registration for every security business and individual guard. Operating without it is a criminal offence.
Security provided only through currently, verifiably PSIRA-registered personnel and partners.
Why this distinction matters

Operating without PSIRA registration carries penalties of up to R10 million and imprisonment of up to 10 years — and procurement evaluators now reject submissions with even one outdated guard certificate. We treat this as non-negotiable, not a checkbox.
